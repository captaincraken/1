How to make a mojito

This is my custom made recepie for doing a delicious mojito for the ones you love at home

Ingredients

  • 1 lime
  • 6 mint leaves
  • 45ml of light rum
  • 120ml of simple syrup
  • 30ml of soda water
  • ice cubes
  • 1 tall glass

Procedure

  1. Cut the lime in 4 parts and put them in the glass
  2. Add the 6 mint leaves and put them in the glass too
  3. Put in the glass all the simple syrup
  4. Muddle the whole thing
  5. Add ice on top
  6. Put in the rum
  7. Now stire the thing
  8. Add the soda

That was my recepie for doing a perfect mojito. I like it sweet but if you like it less sweet simply add less sugar.
